01 / About

Who I Am

I'm an aspiring IT support and networking professional, passionate about understanding how systems and networks work from end to end — from physical cabling to cloud services and DNS resolution. I love breaking things in virtual labs, exploring problems, and rebuilding them to see exactly how each piece interacts.

My approach is simple: learn by doing. Every project I tackle starts with a challenge or a question I don’t yet know the answer to, whether it’s configuring a DHCP server, troubleshooting network issues, or setting up a Linux environment. I document each step to track progress and solidify my understanding.

Currently, I’m building practical skills in networking fundamentals, Linux administration, and IT support workflows through hands-on labs and the Google IT Support Professional Certificate. My goal is to earn certifications like CompTIA A+ and move into a junior support or network role, bringing both technical knowledge and problem-solving mindset to the workplace.
